POP'IT BRAND FILES - START HERE
================================

YOU ONLY NEED TO WORK IN THIS FOLDER:

  C:\Users\FLYGOON\popit\public\brand\

LOGO FILES  -->  put in the "logo" folder:
  - popit-mark.png   (P icon only, transparent background)
  - popit-mark.svg
  - popit-wordmark.svg
  - popit-logo.png

WELCOME BACKGROUND  -->  put in the "animation" folder:
  - welcome-hero.jpg   (concert / crowd photo)

HOW TO ADD FILES:
  1. Double-click OPEN-BRAND-FOLDER.bat on your Desktop or in the popit folder
  2. Drag your exported files into the logo or animation folder
  3. Double-click UPDATE-BRAND.bat
  4. Refresh browser at http://localhost:3000 (Ctrl+Shift+R)

You do NOT need to use code or terminal for normal updates.
Just drag files + double-click UPDATE-BRAND.bat

Same folders also exist on your Desktop at:
  Desktop\popit\public\brand\
(Either location works after running UPDATE-BRAND.bat)
